# Devtrack-Ai
Your Career Memory System.

DevTrack AI is an intelligent platform that helps professionals log their daily work, track contributions, and instantly generate structured, resume-ready summaries.

Instead of struggling to remember what you worked on during job switches or appraisals, DevTrack AI keeps a structured record of your professional growth.

⸻

🎯 Why DevTrack AI?

Professionals often:
	•	Forget important contributions made over months or years
	•	Write generic resume points
	•	Struggle to quantify their impact
	•	Miss mentioning technologies or responsibilities

DevTrack AI solves this by transforming your daily work into structured, measurable, and professionally written career summaries.

⸻
